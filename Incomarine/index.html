About Us

About Us

Who Are We?

Welcome to Incomarine Shipyard, a trusted shipbuilding and repair company with a legacy of excellence. With our skilled workforce, advanced facilities, and adherence to quality, we offer comprehensive services for shipbuilding, repair, and maintenance. From commercial ships to naval vessels, we cater to diverse industries, ensuring superior craftsmanship and reliable solutions.

Our strong client partnerships, commitment to sustainability, and global reputation make us the preferred choice in the maritime industry. Explore our website to learn more about our services and capabilities. We look forward to working with you for your maritime success.

Services

Our Services

Ship Design

From concept to detailed engineering, our team of naval architects and engineers excels in crafting innovative and efficient ship designs tailored to your vision and specifications.

Ship Construction

With state-of-the-art facilities and a skilled workforce, we specialize in constructing high-quality vessels that meet international standards and regulations.

Vessel Maintenance

Our comprehensive maintenance services ensure that your vessels operate at peak performance and remain in optimal condition throughout their lifecycle.

Refit and Repair

We have expertise in revitalizing and enhancing the functionality of existing vessels through our professional refit and repair services, extending their lifespan and improving their capabilities.

Marine Engineering

Our skilled engineers provide innovative solutions for marine systems, propulsion, electrical integration, and other critical engineering aspects of vessel construction.

Project Management

Our dedicated project management team ensures seamless coordination, on-time delivery, and efficient execution of shipbuilding projects from start to finish.
